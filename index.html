Projects

Work

Comparative Analysis of Summarization Models on Amazon Fine Food Reviews
Python | VS Code, NVIDIA RTX A4000
This project evaluates the summarization capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs) using the Amazon Fine Food Reviews dataset from Kaggle. By comparing GPT-2, T5-BASE, BART-LARGE, and FalconSAI/TEXT-SUMMARIZATION, the objective is to identify the model that delivers the most precise summaries of customer reviews. Performance is assessed using the ROGUE metric. Fine-tuning of the models is being conducted using an NVIDIA RTX A4000 to enhance their performance further, aiming to generate even more tailored and accurate summaries.

SpamBot - your daily activity summarizer
Python, Streamlit, Conda, gemini-pro | VS Code
SpamBot simplifies your life by summarizing daily activities from a transcript.txt file, providing both question-answering (QA) and summarization capabilities.
